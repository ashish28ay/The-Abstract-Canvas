The Artist

Behind the Canvas

Ashish is a self-taught abstract artist based in India whose work explores the intersection of emotion, memory, and color. Drawing from everyday moments and inner landscapes, each painting is an act of translation — feelings that resist language finding form in texture and hue.

His practice spans fluid abstracts, bold geometric compositions, and deeply textured works, all created by hand on premium cotton canvas using professional-grade acrylics and mixed media. No two pieces are alike — every brushstroke carries intention.

"I don't paint what I see. I paint what I feel when I close my eyes."
